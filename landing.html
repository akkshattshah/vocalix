About Us

Vocalix is a voice-first writing tool that lives quietly on your desktop. Press a hotkey, speak naturally, and polished text appears wherever your cursor is — emails, documents, search bars, chat apps, anywhere. No switching windows, no copy-pasting, no friction.

Say "Hey Vocalix" and it becomes your writing assistant: draft emails, compose messages, or generate content from a single spoken instruction. Built for speed, privacy, and a premium feel — Vocalix stays out of your way until the moment you need it.
